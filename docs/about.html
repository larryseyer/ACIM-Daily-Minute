About ACIM Daily Minute

What is A Course in Miracles?

A Course in Miracles (ACIM) is a self-study spiritual thought system that teaches the way to universal love and peace through forgiveness. First published in 1976, it consists of three parts:

The Text

The theoretical foundation — 31 chapters exploring the nature of reality, the ego, forgiveness, and the journey to inner peace.

The Workbook

365 lessons — one for each day of the year — designed as practical exercises to shift perception from fear to love.

Manual for Teachers

Answers to common questions and a framework for extending the Course's principles into daily life and relationships.

"Nothing real can be threatened. Nothing unreal exists. Herein lies the peace of God."

What ACIM Daily Minute Does

ACIM Daily Minute is a free public service that delivers the teachings of A Course in Miracles in small, accessible daily doses — through audio, video, and text. Our goal is simple: make it easy for anyone to engage with the Course, one day at a time.

We produce three content streams, each offering a different way to experience the Course:

♨ Daily Minute

A one-minute reading from the ACIM Text — a randomly selected passage delivered as audio and video, seven days a week. Each passage is approximately 130-150 words, drawn from across the entire Text. With roughly 2,100 passages in the corpus, the Daily Minute cycle spans nearly six years before any passage repeats.

✎ Daily Lessons

The 365 Workbook Lessons delivered sequentially, one per weekday (Monday through Friday). Each lesson is read aloud and presented as text, following the Course's own numbering. The complete cycle takes approximately 1.5 years to work through all 365 lessons.

★ Text Series Coming Soon

The complete ACIM Text, read aloud chapter by chapter, section by section. This deep-dive series will begin after all 365 Daily Lessons have been completed. When active, it will run daily, seven days a week.

How It Works

Every day, the day's content is selected, professional-quality audio is generated, video with on-screen text is created, and everything is published to YouTube, TikTok, and this website — delivered faithfully, every day.

All content is produced and delivered at no cost to listeners. There are no ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls, and no tracking. The project is funded entirely by voluntary contributions.

"Teach only love, for that is what you are."

Our Mission

ACIM Daily Minute exists to make A Course in Miracles accessible to anyone who seeks it. We believe the Course's teachings on forgiveness, love, and inner peace are too valuable to be gated behind paywalls or buried in complexity.

By offering the Course in small daily portions — just one minute at a time — we hope to make it approachable for newcomers and a daily companion for longtime students.
