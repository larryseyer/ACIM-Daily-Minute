Support ACIM Daily Minute

The Course should be available to everyone. Your support makes that possible.

Why This Matters

ACIM Daily Minute delivers the teachings of A Course in Miracles freely — no ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls. Every reading, every audio recording, every video is available to anyone who seeks it, at no cost.

But producing this content does have real costs. Professional-quality audio is generated daily using AI voice synthesis. Videos are created and uploaded to YouTube and TikTok. This website and its data infrastructure are maintained to serve readers and listeners around the world.

"To give and to receive are one in truth."

What Your Contribution Supports

🎤 Audio Production

ElevenLabs AI voice synthesis generates natural, professional-quality readings for every passage and lesson, every day.

🌐 Hosting & Infrastructure

Website hosting, data storage, content delivery, and the automated pipeline that keeps everything running without interruption.

📹 Video Production

Daily video creation with on-screen text and audio, published to YouTube and TikTok for maximum accessibility.
